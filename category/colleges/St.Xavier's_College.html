About St. Xavier's College

  1. St. Xavier's College is a private, Catholic, autonomous higher education college under Calcutta University run by the Calcutta Province of the Society of Jesus in Kolkata, India
  2. It was founded by the Jesuits in 1860 and named after St. Francis Xavier, a Jesuit saint of the 16th century, who travelled to India
  3. In 2006, it became the first autonomous college in West Bengal, India, and is affiliated to the University of Calcutta.
  4. The National Institutional Ranking Framework has ranked the college 4th in India in 2021[3] and 8th in 2022
  5. 30 Park Street (now Mother Teresa Sarani), Kolkata-700016, was where the Sans Souci theatre was located, before 1843.
  6. A fire broke out in 1843, leaving nothing but ashes.
  7. This same address was later bought by 7 Jesuits, who had arrived from Belgium. It is here, that the present day campus stands tall.

  8. The present 30, Park Street address where the college is situated, is an amalgamation of numbers 10 and 11 of Park Street. Premise number 11, was bought for Rs 45,000.00, by Fr. Depelchen.
