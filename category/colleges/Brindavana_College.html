INFORMATION

  1. Brindavan College of Engineering (also referred to as BrCE) is an engineering college in Bangalore, Karnataka, India.
  2. The college is affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum[1] and approved by the AICTE, New Delhi
  3. BrCE offers a four-year graduate programme in engineering leading to the award of the degree Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.).
  4. The college postgraduate programmes in Master of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Computer Applications (MCA).
  5. The campus is 1 km from Baglur cross, on the way to Baglur on Hyderabad-Bangalore NH 7.
  6. BrCE was started by Dr. Majed A.A. Sabha, in 2008 after the success of Brindavan Group of Institutions in 1992. B.R Shetty Venture.
  7. The library has online search facilities, a Book Bank, and an inter-library loan facility with British Council Library, Bangalore and NAL, Bangalore.
  8. Separate hostels for boys and girls are within the campus. Both hostels house seniors as well as junior under same hostel.

Why it is Popular?

Brindavan College of UG/PG is a private institute featured among Top Colleges in Bangalore. The college is popularly known for its undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various streams such as Commerce, Management, Science and Arts. The admissions to all undergraduate courses are granted on the basis of merit in the last qualifying examinations. Candidates applying for MBA and MCA need to appear for the state level entrance exams in order to be eligible.Brindavan College carries out a number of research activities every year in Commerce, Management and Computer Science streams. Additionally, the institute also started extension programmes to sensitize the students to social problems.
