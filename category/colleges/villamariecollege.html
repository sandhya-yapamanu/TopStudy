About Villa Marie College

  1. Villa Marie Intermediate, Degree and Post Graduate College (established in 1991 in Hyderabad, India) is an educational institution which was started by Dr. Y. Philomena, Founder and Director.
  2. Admission into any undergraduate course can be secured by passing in intermediate or other equivalent programs with 60% and above marks
  3. The classrooms are well furnished and properly ventilated. Projectors are available for the Degree and MBA classrooms
  4. The Seminar Hall[5] is an air-conditioned venue with seating capacity for more than 200 students. There is one on every floor equipped with a public address system and an LCD projector.
  5. The Media and Research centre is equipped with a soundproof audio visual mixing studio.
  6. It has a photography unit, editing tables and computers with the latest design software.
  7. There is a complete non-linear editing setup available to encourage film-making.
  8. Students are encouraged to take part in various co-curricular activities which are job oriented. This may open doors of talent and art that covers once in a year in the form of Kala, an exhibition of art which also allows the sale of handmade items.
