About Bharata Mata College

  1. Bharata Mata College is an institution of higher education, located in Thrikkakara, Kochi, in the south Indian state of Kerala.
  2. The college is affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University[2] and offers 17 undergraduate, 4 post-graduate and 2 professional programmes.
  3. The main campus also houses three research centres in Chemistry, Mathematics and Commerce and operates as the offcampus centre for the School of Distance Education of the Mahatma Gandhi University.
  4. It holds A+ grade accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) since 2019.
  5. Bharata Mata College was founded by Joseph Parecattil under the aegis of Bharata Mata Educational Trust of the Archdiocese of Ernakulam Angamaly on 16 June 1965 as a junior college offering five pre-degree courses.
  6. The founding management was headed by Monsignor Varghese Kavalakatt as the manager and S. H. Antony CMI as the principal.
  7. Bharata Mata School of Legal Studies, Bharata Mata Institute of Management[4] and Bharata Mata School of Social Work[5] are sister institutions of the college, functioning under Bharata Mata Educational Trust.
